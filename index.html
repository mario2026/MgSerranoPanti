A Beginners Guide

Bloons TD 6 is a 2018 tower defense video game. It is the sixth game in the Bloons TD series. It was developed and published by Ninja Kiwi.

In Bloons TD 6, the player creates a defense to pop all of the enemy balloons. These enemy balloons are called "Bloons", which must be destroyed by popping them with Monkeys. Popping Bloons rewards cash to spend on more defense. Cash is spent to buy Monkeys and their upgrades.

If the Bloons reach the exit, they will cause the player to lose lives. Lose a certain amount of lives in a difficulty, and you lose. However, if you survive until the final round depending on the difficulty, you win.
